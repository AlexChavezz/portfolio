# MONGODB ATLAS

#### What is mongodb atlas

Atlas is a MongoDB cloud-based developer data platform and its core is an atlas database or *database as a service* (DBaaS), this means that you should don't care about maintenance infrastructure due to atlas do it for us.

#### Database deployments 

Atlas provide two kinds of database deployments such as:

*Serverless*: A serverless instance scales on demand and only charges you for the resources that you use.
Serverless instances are in general a great option for an application with a highly variable or sparse workloads. 


*Cluster*: A cluster is a several mongoDB servers working together, there are two cluster options *shared* and *dedicated*.
A shared clusters are smaller and are included in the free tier.
